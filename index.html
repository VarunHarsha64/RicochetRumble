RICOCHET RUMBLE

8x8 Board Game Rules

🎮 Objective

🏆 Destroy the opponent's Titan.

🧩 Pieces

  • 🎴 Titan | 🛡️ Tank | 🌀 Ricochet (x2)
  • ↩️ Semi Ricochet (x2) | 🎯 Cannon

📏 Moves

  • Any piece: Move 1 tile or rotate.
  • Cannon: Horizontal moves only.

🔄 Abilities

  • 🌀 Ricochets: Reflect 90°; swap/rotate.
  • ↩️ Semi Ricochets: Reflect 90° on one side; break on the other.
  • 🛡️ Tanks: Absorb bullets except one side.

⏳ Timer

  • Each player: 10 minutes.
  • Switch for bullet travel time.

⏸️ Controls

  • Pause ⏸️ | Reset 🔄 | Continue ▶️
  • Redo 🔁 | Undo 🔄

📝 History

📜 Shows move history.

🧙 Spell Slider

🪄 Bullets bypass Tanks and Semi Ricochets when on.
